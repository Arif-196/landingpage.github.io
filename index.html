About Point Blank


Point Blank is a computer shooting game that is played online.

This FPS genre game tells the story of the feud between the Free Rebels and the government, in this case the Counter Terrorist Force (CT-Force).

This game was developed by Zepetto from South Korea and published by Zepetto. In Indonesia, Point Blank was initially released by PT Kreon through Gemscool in 2009. Point Blank's service at Gemscool ran until the contract with Zepetto ended in June 2015.

Was adrift for a month due to server shutdown, moreover it was reported that this game would be closed by Gemscool, on June 30, 2015 Point Blank was again launched by the developer Garena Indonesia With the re-launch of Point Blank by the new developer, Garena is determined to improve the comfort of playing the game, as well as because Garena Indonesia's Point Blank has replaced it with Point Blank Evolution.
